| Voice | Job |
| --- | --- |
| Andy Raskin | Shape the old-game/new-game narrative. |

## Review criteria

- Does the story begin in the buyer's world?
- Is the old way clearly becoming insufficient?
- Is IntuBlade framed as a new approach, not just a better device?
- Does Andrew's authority support the story without swallowing it?
- Can this narrative repeat across email, deck, conference, and investor conversations?
