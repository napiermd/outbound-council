# Hospital Sales Pack

Use this pack for hospital systems, emergency departments, anesthesia, value analysis committees, procurement, clinical leadership, and enterprise pilots.

## Default voices

- John McMahon
- Mark Copeland
- Josh Makower
- Bob Wachter
- Lisa Suennen
- Value Analysis Director archetype
- Supply Chain / Procurement Lead archetype
- Market Access and Health Economics Playbook

## Optional voices

- April Dunford for positioning and category clarity
- Matt Dixon for no-decision risk
- Howie Chan for physician choice and brand trust
- ED Medical Director / Airway Lead for ED adoption
- Anesthesia Chair / Chief CRNA for OR or offsite anesthesia
- Respiratory Therapy Director for RT workflow and airway support
- Infection Prevention Lead for single-use/reprocessing claims
- Relevant clinical airway voice for clinical credibility
- Relevant `competitive/` profile for named competitors or substitutes

- qualification
- economic buyer clarity
- clinical champion development
- value analysis and procurement gates
- evidence and workflow fit
- pilot design
- institutional trust
- buyer-specific committee readiness
- economic-case defensibility

## Voice jobs

| Voice | Job |
| --- | --- |
| John McMahon | Inspect qualification, power, champion strength, and next commitment. |
| Mark Copeland | Map value-analysis, supply chain, risk, and procurement gates. |
| Josh Makower | Test whether the need, workflow, product, and business model fit. |
| Bob Wachter | Stress-test clinical workflow, safety, and implementation realism. |
| Lisa Suennen | Pressure-test market, partnership, business-model, and health-system strategy. |
| Value Analysis Director | Check evidence, comparator, risk, and committee readiness. |
| Supply Chain / Procurement | Check SKU, stocking, vendor, contract, and trial-to-purchase path. |
| Market Access Playbook | Check economic case, budget-impact assumptions, and value dossier. |

## Review criteria

- Is the economic buyer known?
- Is there a clinical champion and a value-analysis path?
- Are clinical claims supported and bounded?
- Does the pilot have success criteria and a decision date?
- Is there a real next buyer commitment?
- Does the economic case name a comparator and adoption gate?
- Are infection prevention, RT, supply chain, and clinical leadership included when relevant?
- Is the competitor/substitute response safe and specific?
