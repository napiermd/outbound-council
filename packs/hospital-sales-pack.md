# Hospital Sales Pack

Use this pack for hospital systems, emergency departments, anesthesia, value analysis committees, procurement, clinical leadership, and enterprise pilots.

## Default voices

- John McMahon
- Mark Copeland
- Josh Makower
- Bob Wachter
- Lisa Suennen

## Optional voices

- April Dunford for positioning and category clarity
- Matt Dixon for no-decision risk
- Howie Chan for physician choice and brand trust

## What this pack optimizes for

- qualification
- economic buyer clarity
- clinical champion development
- value analysis and procurement gates
- evidence and workflow fit
- pilot design
- institutional trust

## Common output

- deal review
- pilot plan
- hospital buying-map
- value-analysis narrative
- champion enablement note
- clinical evidence checklist

## Load from each profile

- `IntuBlade applications`
- `Signature questions`
- `What this voice would push on`
- `Voice extraction targets`

## Voice jobs

| Voice | Job |
| --- | --- |
| John McMahon | Inspect qualification, power, champion strength, and next commitment. |
| Mark Copeland | Map value-analysis, supply chain, risk, and procurement gates. |
| Josh Makower | Test whether the need, workflow, product, and business model fit. |
| Bob Wachter | Stress-test clinical workflow, safety, and implementation realism. |
| Lisa Suennen | Pressure-test market, partnership, business-model, and health-system strategy. |

## Review criteria

- Is the economic buyer known?
- Is there a clinical champion and a value-analysis path?
- Are clinical claims supported and bounded?
- Does the pilot have success criteria and a decision date?
- Is there a real next buyer commitment?
