# EMS Outbound Pack

Use this pack for fire departments, EMS agencies, medical directors, training officers, and regional public-safety clusters.

## Default voices

- April Dunford
- Bob Moesta
- Zed Williamson
- Howie Chan
- Matt Dixon

## Optional voices

- Omar Khateeb for medtech founder-led narrative
- John McMahon for high-value departments or multi-stakeholder deal inspection
- Bob Wachter when the message includes clinical AI, procedural guidance, or hospital workflow claims

## What this pack optimizes for

- awareness-stage buyers
- risk removal
- peer credibility
- deployment-kit acceptance
- reply-worthy operational questions
- low-friction next steps

## Common output

- cold email
- follow-up sequence
- signal opener
- deployment kit framing
- reply handling
- county or medical-director cluster strategy

## Load from each profile

- `IntuBlade applications`
- `Signature questions`
- `What this voice is suspicious of`
- `Voice extraction targets`

## Voice jobs

| Voice | Job |
| --- | --- |
| April Dunford | Keep the category and competitive frame clear. |
| Bob Moesta | Identify the struggling moment and switching trigger. |
| Zed Williamson | Catch device-sales adoption blockers. |
| Howie Chan | Make the message memorable and trustworthy. |
| Matt Dixon | Reduce decision risk and avoid too many choices. |

## Review criteria

- Does the opener prove real research?
- Does the email name a reason to change now?
- Does it make the deployment kit feel low-risk and valuable?
- Does the message avoid sounding like a vendor blast?
- Is the question concrete enough for a chief to answer quickly?
