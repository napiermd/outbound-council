## Why this source matters

This current episode listing focuses on market category as a tool for customer understanding, not a replacement for messaging. It warns that aspirational categories can create false assumptions that sales and marketing must undo. That is central for IntuBlade: a new category frame may help, but only if it clarifies the buying context.

## Frameworks

- Market category as context: category helps buyers understand what assumptions to bring.
- Three positioning strategies: choose whether to use an existing category, subcategory, or new category based on best chance to win.
- Wrong category creates false assumptions and sales friction.

## Objections and warnings

- Do not use category language that makes the buyer ask basic "what is this?" questions.
- Do not force software/AI positioning onto buyers who first need device adoption clarity.
