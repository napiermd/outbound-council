## Why this source matters

This article explicitly ties medical device case growth to decision science and psychology. It warns that flashy tactics may not change behavior and argues that selling is less about pitching benefits and more about helping someone make a decision. That is an IntuBlade-relevant operating rule for EMS and hospital adoption.
