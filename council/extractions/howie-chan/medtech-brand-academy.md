## Why this source matters

The academy page is the richest public source for Howie's medtech brand framework. It lays out modules on brand foundations, positioning, brand architecture, personality, creative platform, activation, product marketecture, ingredient branding, and company brand. It also states that brand strategy matters at inflection points such as stealth exit, funding, IPO, acquisition integration, product launch, and thought leadership.

## Frameworks

- SPARK framework for brand first principles.
- RAD positioning framework and 4Cs.
- Brand architecture spectrum for portfolios and acquisitions.
- Creative platform: visual, verbal, and experiential brand language.
- PESO activation: paid, earned, shared, owned channels.
- Product marketecture: communicate what people buy, not just what you sell.

## Language patterns

- "Brand strategy" is treated as a commercial operating system, not cosmetic design.
- "Strategic brand leader" is the role Howie wants marketers to take.
- "Marketecture" links product complexity to buyer comprehension.

## Stories and examples
