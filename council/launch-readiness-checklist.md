# Launch Readiness Checklist

Use this after council changes or before major GTM work.

## Structural checks

- Clinical airway profiles include canonical sources, current signals, IntuBlade applications, source mining backlog, voice extraction targets, and last reviewed.
- Buyer archetypes include jobs-to-be-done, buying triggers, objections, proof needed, language patterns, and outbound review criteria.
- Competitive profiles include source links, positioning, claims, buyer appeal, IntuBlade counter-positioning, evidence gaps/risks, and outbound-safe language.
- `source-index.md`, `roster.md`, packs, and invocation patterns reference the new layers.

## Scenario checks

- EMS cold email: load `packs/ems-outbound-pack.md`, EMS buyer archetypes, and one clinical airway voice if clinical claims are present.
- Hospital deal: load `packs/hospital-sales-pack.md`, value-analysis/procurement/infection-prevention archetypes, market-access playbook, and relevant competitor profile.
- Competitor-facing pitch: load `competitive/` profile, April Dunford, Christopher Lochhead, and buyer archetype.
- Procedural-guidance AI claim: load `packs/procedural-guidance-ai-pack.md`, Bob Wachter, Scott Weingart, Maia Dorsett, Josh Makower, and market-access playbook.

## Quality checks

- Search for stale placeholders: `pending profile`, `TODO`, empty source sections.
- Spot-check representative source links.
- Confirm no PHI or private customer identifiers are introduced.
- Keep direct buyer language separate from inference in `field-signals/`.
